About Us

Our Love Story Begins

Two hearts, two souls, two paths that crossed by fate and destiny. What started as a chance meeting has blossomed into the most beautiful love story we could have ever imagined.

We've shared countless adventures, weathered life's storms together, and discovered that we're stronger as one. Now, we're ready to take the next step in our journey together.

Join us as we celebrate our love and commitment to each other, surrounded by family and friends.

Our Journey

2016

First Meeting

Our first meeting was at Rachael’s school hostel. What was meant to be just a brief encounter turned into an exchange of contacts, hours of conversation, shared study sessions, and the start of something truly special- A story we would write together.

2016

First Date

Our very first official date was at the university’s favorite Coke spot. What began as a simple outing quickly became unforgettable. Laughter flowed effortlessly, and in that perfect moment together, we both knew we had found something truly extraordinary.

2022

Moving In Together

We took the big step of moving in together. Learning to share a space, merge our belongings, and create a home together strengthened our bond even more.

2023

The Proposal

During the beautiful late spring, at a stunning rooftop venue surrounded by close friends and loved ones, Destiny got down on one knee. With a heart full of love and eyes blissful with happiness, Rachael said YES to forever.
